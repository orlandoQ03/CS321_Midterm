Outcomes of Having a Blog

One of the most important aspects of a well written blog is being able to communicate what your brand is. Some of the outcomes that result from this are as follows (levelled by importance):

  1. Engagement
    Uploaded 2/20/2024

    It is important to keep the audience engaged and updated about certain topics. Whether it is about projects or your daily life, it helps readers connect with the author more.

      
  2. Impression
    Uploaded 2/20/2024

    When you write a blog that is both personal and professional, it will give clients and possibly employers a good impression about you. This will help you stand out as a brand as well as a person.

      
  3. Expertise

    Writing a blog gives you the chance to showcase your portfolio. This can be a place to show off your code along with a github link. Or, if you're more of a cooking type, you can show off your delicious recipies.

      
  4. Marketing

    If you're selling a product or service, this is a great way to advertise yourself. Especially as a Web Developer, you can impress clients your design skills. For whatever you write your blogs for, it is basically an advertisment for your brand.

      
  5. Networking

    One of the most important aspects of a great blog is creating a platform where employeers can reach out to you. Combined with engagement, impression, and expertise, clients would love to have lunch with you.

      
  6. Personal Development

    After having done everything, these blogs also serve as something to reflect on. To a more personal level, the author can look back and see how much they have improved so far. This will further them into their career and in their personal life.
